I-Go (围棋) is an oriental strategic board game for two players (see details: Go (game) - Wikipedia). And Trigo is the 3D extension of Go. Our project ‘K.L.'s Trigo’ is a platform for Trigo game play.

K.L.'s Trigo is an open-source project, see the home page on SourceForge.net.
